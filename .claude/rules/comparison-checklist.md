# Comparison Checklist

When comparing your screenshot against the reference image, check for mismatches in:

- **Spacing and padding** - Measure in px, verify margins and gaps
- **Font sizes, weights, and line heights** - Check typography hierarchy
- **Colors** - Use exact hex values, verify all color variations
- **Alignment and positioning** - Check element placement and layout
- **Border radii, shadows, and effects** - Verify visual effects match
- **Responsive behavior** - Test at different breakpoints
- **Image/icon sizing and placement** - Ensure correct dimensions and positions

## Reporting Mismatches

Be specific when documenting differences:
- ✅ "Heading is 32px but reference shows ~24px"
- ✅ "Gap between cards is 16px but should be 24px"
- ❌ "Font looks too big"
- ❌ "Spacing is off"
